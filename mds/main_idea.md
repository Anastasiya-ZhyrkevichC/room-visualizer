Main idea is to create a web site, which will be used for selling the kitchens.

The main problem for all the furniture sellers is that you cannot see the price of your product before you reach out to the company manager. Once you reached out to the manager, your number is inside the potential customers, and the sales manager starts calling you. And the customer cannot calculate the price of the kitchen without reaching the manager, because the magic formula (for calculating the final product price) is somewhere in the manager's program and databases.

I want to create a pure process, where the customer can construct their own kitchen out of modules we provide and they will see the price for each of the module (based of the size and the material), Calculate the price and see what could be changed to make the project cheaper or more expensive.

Basic kitchen modules are some basic kitchen boards of the standart size.
